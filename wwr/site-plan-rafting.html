Overview

Purpose

We offer the grand advanture to white water rafting in Glacier National Park and Flathead lake. We want to share our experienced and sense of adventure in the wide open space of Northwestern Montana. We offer adventure levels for all experience levels, from beginning to advanced. We want to share a love of the nature, wildlife we have in Kalispell, MT.

Audience

Kalispell Rafting Company is your starting point for adventures in Glacier National Park and Flathead Lake. We offer half-day, full -day and multi-day adventures on the senic Middle Fork and North Fork of the Flathead River. Bordering the legendary Glacier National Park. Our paddle powered excursions offer a chance to connect to the untamed beauty of the Montana scenery. We provide whitewhite pros to guide and share their experence with everylevel of rafters.
